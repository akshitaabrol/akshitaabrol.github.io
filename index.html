// 01 · Research Focus

AI-Native Clinical Intelligence

My research sits at the intersection of signal processing and multimodal AI, applied to clinical and biomedical problems. I design systems that can reliably interpret speech, vision, and network data in real-world, high-throughput settings — not just in controlled lab conditions.

My doctoral work produced the first Hindi-language schizophrenic and emotional speech corpus, alongside CNN- and fractal-based classification methods that earned two international best paper awards. This has since expanded into a broader programme in multimodal AI, GenAI-powered clinical automation validated with hospital partners, and privacy-preserving federated learning for neurological diagnostics.

As a faculty member, I aim to build a research group training students to design AI systems at the systems level — robust, explainable, and ready for deployment in healthcare and education.

// 02 · Featured Projects

Research & Industry Projects

Clinical AI · Computer Vision

AutoVA — Automated Visual Acuity Testing

AI-powered visual acuity assessment developed with Tan Tock Seng Hospital. Integrates robust audio-visual speech recognition for automated, clinician-grade eye testing in noisy clinical environments — removing reliance on manual charting.

Pilot Deployed · TTSHAPSIPA ASC 2025IALP 2025

Partner: Tan Tock Seng Hospital, Singapore

Federated Learning · Neurological AI

Parkinson's Disease Federated Learning AI

Privacy-preserving, explainable AI framework for Parkinson's Disease diagnosis using federated learning on MRI data across distributed clinical sites. Addresses data-sharing barriers in healthcare AI while maintaining diagnostic accuracy and model transparency.

ACM MM I2M Workshop 2025APSIPA ASC 2026 (submitted)

Institution: SIT × NVIDIA AI Centre (SNAIC)

Speech AI · Psychiatric Assessment

Schizophrenic Speech Corpus & Classification

Created the first Hindi-language schizophrenic and emotional speech corpus. Developed CNN, GMM, and fractal analysis methods for automated schizophrenia classification from speech, enabling non-invasive, scalable mental health screening.

Sydney R. Parker Best Paper 2021M.N.S. Swamy Best Paper 2021CSSP, Springer

Venue: Circuits, Systems & Signal Processing, Springer

EdTech AI · LLM Applications

ForumAId — GenAI for Academic LMS

GenAI-powered tool integrated into Learning Management Systems to enhance student–instructor interaction. Reduces instructor load on repetitive queries while surfacing meaningful student engagement signals, validated in live SIT deployment.

5G/6G · Congestion Control

AI-Assisted Traffic Routing & BBR for 6G

GNN-based Deep Reinforcement Learning for adaptive traffic routing in next-generation networks. Incorporates edge intelligence principles for low-latency inference at network endpoints. Re-engineered BBR congestion control (FaiRTT) for 5G-Advanced/6G environments. Accompanied by a comprehensive survey in ACM Computing Surveys 2026.
